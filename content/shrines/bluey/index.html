"Wait, isn't that a kid's show?"

Bluey is an Australian children's TV program.
you know what Bluey is. even if you know little to nothing about it, it's basically impossible to avoid lol

Bluey is a show/cartoon about a family of Heelers, centered around, of course, Bluey, a Blue Heeler! as well as her little sister, Bingo, her mum, Chilli, and her dad, Bandit!

despite being a TV show aimed at children, it has insane popularity with not-children too, and I am one of those as it turns out!

and by that i mean i'm obsessed. biscuits.

COCONUTS HAVE WATER IN THEM!

yeah uh, i kinda became hyperfixated on this show starting in July (2025). i randomly decided to try it on Disney+ and got into it pretty quickly
